Our story: rooted in unity

Wagagai Coffee Farmers' SACCO was founded in 2021 by a group of 10 farmers and small-scale traders who believed in collective financial strength. What started as a modest savings circle has grown into one of the most trusted cooperative financial institutions in the Mt. Elgon region.

Over the years, our SACCO has stayed true to the cooperative principle of "people helping people." We have weathered economic shifts, embraced digital transformation, and expanded membership to over 600 active members. Our mission remains clear: provide affordable credit, promote savings culture, and invest in member well-being.

Our history is a story of resilience and member-first innovation. Today, we proudly serve farmers, entrepreneurs, and professionals while preserving the spirit of mutual assistance.
